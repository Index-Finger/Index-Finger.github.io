My Target Translation
Yellow flowers,
Arrayed in grasses,
Sway in winds.
Bitter men come
With terrible fury see
Dainty dandelions.
Ripping, tugging,
Clear cutting life,
Till everywhere, green.
Rain salves, sun soothes,
Warming the ground till
Burgeoning life inhales.
It looks around and,
Blooms,
Yellow flower.
Delicate bees bobble in, pollinate.
Flowers sway in winds.
With night they fall away,
with morning they rise,
again and again, till,
Puff! They pop, white,
And sway in the wind:
Flower flags.
Bitter men come,
“there!”
Peaceful flowers,
Sway in winds.
Hands rip,
Flowers pop
And spread.
